# Deep-Dive Analysis: Customer Segmentation

## Business Question
Which customers contribute most to sales and profit?

## Segmentation Logic
- High Value: Customer sales above average customer sales.
- Medium Value: Customer sales around average.
- Low Value: Customer sales below average.

## Key Findings
- High-value customers contributed the highest sales and profit.
- Medium-value customers represented growth opportunities.
- Low-value customers made up a large portion of the customer base but generated lower profits.

## Recommendations
- Retain high-value customers with loyalty programs.
- Upsell medium-value customers.
- Use targeted campaigns to increase low-value customer purchases.
